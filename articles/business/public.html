How To Register a Public Limited Company On Ecitizen -Ebusiness

Below is what you need to know about Local Public Limited Company as per the New Companies Act 2015.

In the new Act, a Public Limited Company is defined under Section 10 to mean;

  • A company whose articles permit its members to the right to transfer their shares in the company
  • A company whose incorporation or formation states that is a public company

The process of registering a public limited company shall be completed upon meeting of the following requirements.

  • Company name proposed for name search approval.
  • Company objectives. The nature of Business and activities the company will undertake.
  • Names of the shareholders in Full.
  • Names of the Directors in full if different from the shareholders.
  • If any of the directors are foreigners then a Kenyan director and shareholder is needed.
  • Postal and Email address and Telephone Numbers of the shareholders and Directors.
  • Number of shares to be held by each shareholder.
  • Proposed Company’s physical address and the Directors. The Physical Address should include road, plot number, town and county.
  • Copy of ID / Passport of the Shareholders / Directors.
  • Passport photos of the Shareholders / Directors.
  • Copy of itax PIN certificate of the Shareholders / Directors.

The documents to be filed for registration are CR 1, CR 2, CR8, CR 10, CR 12 and Statement of Nominal Capital

Under Section 516 a public limited company cannot be issued with a trading certificate unless it meets the minimum Kshs 6,750,000 nominal value of a public listed company’s allotted share capital. The Public Limited Company should have a Company Secretary at all times.

Initial Nominal capital is exempt from payment of Stamp duty.

Requirements On Registration

  • An account with E-citizen (In case you don’t have one you can check our tutorial on how to register on E-citizen).
  • At least three potential unique names for your Private Limited Company to be.
  • Ksh 10,650 (exclusive of any transaction fees).

Step 1

Open the Ecitizen sign in page using the following linkhttps://accounts.ecitizen.go.ke/login and you will find yourself on the following page.

Once you have been logged in, click on Business Registration Services to proceed.

You will be prompted to log in again. Just input your log in detail again and click the log in button.

Step 2

Click on Make Application to proceed.

On the page that follows click on Apply now under Public Limited Company.

A small window shows up explaining what a Public Limited Company, just click on Apply Now to proceed.

Step3

On the first page that shows up, enter at least your first three preferred name for your company. They have to be unique and decent.

Click save and continue once you are have filled all your desired company names.

Step 4

On the page that follows, start by filling up the applicant’s details i.e. their capacity in the company and their postal address. Next fill in the application details starting with a choice on articles of association.

Fill in the nature of business your company will be dealing with and then choose the primary business activities of your business from the choices given. Fill in the target start date of your company, number of employees you intend to employ on starting the company whose salary is more than Kshs 13,486 monthly and the estimated turnover of your company annually. Choose whether your company is a branch of another company and if it is formed as a result of amalgamation or acquisition.

Click Save and Continue to proceed.

Step 5

On the page that follows, fill in the registered address of your company. Click on Save and Continue button to proceed.

Step 6

On this page, fill in the number of shares your company has and the value of each share. Click on Save and Continue Button to proceed.

Step 7

On this page click on Add Director/Shareholder button to fill in the details of any director or shareholder.

On the window that shows up choose the designation of the person whose details you wish to fill in, type their id number and their first name on Id and then click verify. That should auto-fill some basic information about the individual. Fill in the contact information, postal address, residential address, share information and beneficial information of the individual. You will be required to attach a passport photo. Click Submit once all the details have been filled accurately.

Do the same for all directors, shareholders and secretaries. Note that a Public Limited Company must have a secretary. Click Save and Continue to proceed.

Step 8

On the page that follows, download the documents that provided there, sign and attach them back. Click Submit and Review to proceed.

Step 9

Make sure every information given is accurate and filled at the right places. Check the box next to the confirmation message and then Click Finish to proceed.

Step 10

On the page, choose the method of payment you prefer to make payment with, click on it, follow the instructions given to make payment and once you receive the confirmation message click on complete to proceed.

On the page that follows, you can print your receipt. At this point you have successfully applied for the registration of your Public Limited Company. The application is under review and you will be contacted on your registered number.

You can keep on check the status of your application on the homepage.
