How To File VAT Nil Returns

You have your business or company and its time to file those monthly returns, VAT returns. Unfortunately you are not sure how to file them and you really donâ€™t want to mess up with the filling of this returns.

In this tutorial I have done my best to guide you on how to file VAT Nil returns. Enjoy.

Requirements

  1. Working Internet Connection
  2. KRA Pin
  3. Password to the KRA web portal. (In case you have forgotten this you can check our tutorial on how to reset your KRA Web Portal Password.)

Step 1

Start by opening the KRA Itax Web Portal using the following link https://itax.kra.go.ke/KRA-Portal/ and you will find yourself in the following page.

Step 2

Enter your KRA Pin number and then click continue.

Enter your password and then do the arithmetic that follows (feel free to use a calculator). Then click the login button.

Step 3

On the page that follows click Returns on the navigation bar above.

From the drop-list that shows click File Nil Return

Step 4

On the page that follows choose Value Added Tax (VAT).

Click next once done.

Step 5

On this page choose the period of return and then click submit.

Step 6

On this page you can download your return receipt by simply clicking the Download Return Receipt link.

You have successfully filled your VAT Nil Returns. Thank you for using our tutorial.
